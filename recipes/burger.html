Burgers

Description

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Bread bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Press a handfull of the beef into a patty shape
  2. Cook the patty in a frying pan over a high heat
  3. Remove the patty to rest, and place the bun cut sides down into the pan to toast
  4. Remove the bun, place a bed of lettuce on the bottom bread followed by the tomato sliced
  5. place the patty onto the tomato then lay the cheese over the top
  6. Spread sauce over the inside of the top bun, then put into the burger to complete
